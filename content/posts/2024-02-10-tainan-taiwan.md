Tainan is different. Older, slower, deeply rooted. This city doesn't rush.

## Temple City

They call it the city of temples for a reason. I stopped counting after visiting my fifth in one afternoon. Each one layered with history, incense smoke hanging in the air.

## Street Food Capital

If Taipei has night markets, Tainan has street food culture as a way of life. Dan zai noodles, coffin bread, shrimp rolls - every corner had something new to try.

## The Slower South

After Taipei's intensity, Tainan felt like a breath. People move differently here. Conversations linger. Time stretches.

## History Everywhere

Old streets, traditional buildings, stories embedded in every alley. This is where Taiwan's history lives.
