Hualien is where Taiwan gets wild. Mountains meet ocean. Marble meets jungle.

## Taroko Gorge

The photos don't do it justice. Marble walls carved by rivers over millennia. Turquoise water. Trails that make you feel microscopic against the scale of nature.

## Pacific Coast

The east coast is dramatic. Black sand, massive cliffs, waves that sound like thunder. Stood on the beach and felt very small.

## Small City Feel

After the gorge tourism, Hualien city itself is quiet. Night market with local food, scooters puttering around, mountains looming in every direction.

## Slower Travel

This leg reminded me to slow down. Not every moment needs to be packed with sights. Sometimes you just need to stare at a gorge for an hour.

Final Taiwan stop before heading to Hong Kong.
