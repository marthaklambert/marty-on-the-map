Hong Kong is density personified. Vertical, layered, intense.

## The Verticality

Everything stacks. Buildings, people, energy. Take an escalator up three floors just to cross the street. The city exists in 3D in a way nowhere else does.

## Dim Sum Mastery

Found a local place in Sham Shui Po. Cart service, tiny plates, tea constantly refilled. The ritual of dim sum hits different here.

## Markets and Malls

The contrast is wild. Luxury malls with Gucci and Prada, then turn a corner into wet markets selling live fish and vegetables. Both worlds coexisting, layered on top of each other.

## Harbor Views

Victoria Peak at sunset. The entire city spread below, lights starting to flicker on. One of those views that makes you stop and just... look.

## Pause Point

Staying here for a bit. Figuring out the next move. Sometimes you need a moment to process where you've been before deciding where you're going.
